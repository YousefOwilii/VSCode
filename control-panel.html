Fire Pump Control Panel

Efficient and compliant fire pump control panel designed for industrial and commercial applications, offering reliable performance, robust construction, and adherence to IEC safety standards.

Our fire pump control panels are engineered to provide dependable control and monitoring for fire suppression systems in demanding industrial and commercial environments. Built with high-quality materials and designed for ease of use, these panels ensure safe and efficient operation when it matters most. With advanced protection features and compliance with IEC standards, our control panels are a reliable choice for facilities prioritizing safety and durability.

Material and Build:

  • Made from high-quality electro-galvanized steel with a sheet thickness of up to 1.2 mm, ensuring durability and resistance to environmental factors.

Finish:

  • Matt-finish polyester powder-coated in RAL-7035 light gray, providing a clean, professional appearance and increased resistance to corrosion.

Compliance:

  • Meets IEC 60529 standards, demonstrating adherence to international safety and quality requirements.

Degree of Protection:

  • IP41 rating, offering protection against solid objects larger than 1mm and limited protection against water ingress.

Power Indicator:

  • Displays the power status of the control panel, ensuring the system is ready for operation.

Run Indicator:

  • Indicates when the pump is actively running, providing a quick visual cue for system activity.

Trip Indicator:

  • Alerts operators to any circuit interruptions, helping to identify and address faults swiftly.

Low-Level Indicator:

  • Notifies the user of low water or fuel levels, which may need attention to ensure continuous operation.

Selector Switch:

  • Allows manual or automatic selection, offering flexibility in operation modes to suit different scenarios.

Relays/Timers with Base:

  • Control the timing and sequence of operations, essential for automating the fire pump system and reducing manual intervention.

Two-Pole Breaker:

  • Provides protection for circuits with lower power requirements, helping to prevent electrical overloads and ensuring system safety.

Three-Pole Breaker:

  • Handles higher power loads, designed to protect the main power circuits from damage due to overcurrent or short circuits.

Contactor:

  • A crucial component for controlling high-power circuits in the panel, the contactor allows for safe switching of the fire pump motor.

Overload Protection:

  • Protects the pump motor from excessive current, reducing the risk of overheating and prolonging the motor’s lifespan.

Terminal Block:

  • Provides an organized and secure connection point for wiring, ensuring all connections are safe and accessible for maintenance.
