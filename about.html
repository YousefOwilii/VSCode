About us

Business Goal:
To get it right the first time, every time and strive to exceed customer expectations whenever possible.

Business Management Process:
Our business system will conform to the requirements of NFPA standards.

Business Principles:
Quality derives from a staff commitment to service and excellence. This belief is stated as business principles.

Customer Focus:
Customer requirements come first. Quality begins and ends with the customer and we strive to always satisfy their needs. This is reflected in our flexibility - both as individuals and as an organization.

Relationships:
We build mutually beneficial relationships with customers and suppliers.

Innovation:
We search for, design and supply creative products and services.

Teamwork:
Our Employees work together as a team to satisfy our customers.

Process Emphasis:
We focus our Employees on practical processes to ensure consistent results.

Continual Improvement:
Through management review of our Business Management System we aim to continually improve business performance, processes, products and services.

Employee Development:
Through active mentoring we create a culture where staff have the skills and are empowered to take responsibility for the results of their actions which contributes to the success of the company.

Organization Chart

This is the organization chart of our company, starting from the General Manager at the top, who oversees the entire operation. Under the General Manager, we have key departments such as Engineering, which includes Marketing & Sales, Tender, Executive, and Quality divisions. These departments handle everything from client relations and project management to technical evaluations. Additionally, there are supporting departments like Financial, Legal Affairs, and Administrative Affairs, which manage finances, legal matters, IT, human resources, and general administration. Each department plays a crucial role in ensuring the company runs smoothly and efficiently.
